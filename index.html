Randomized Cricket Variant

Cricket · Random

Play cricket with targets 1–20 and Bull. Each round surfaces 6 random available targets. If a displayed target is not hit during the round, it gets rerolled from the remaining untouched targets until every target has been hit at least once. Win by closing all targets and finishing with the most points.
